Welcome to the Mystic Oracle Web App!


This user manual will guide you through the features and functionality of our app, allowing you to explore the realms of the past, present, and future through the wisdom of tarot cards. Let's get started!
  1. your preferred web browser and navigate to the Mystic Oracle website. Once the website loads, you will find the main interface with three buttons labeled "Past," "Present," and "Future."

  2. Choosing a Time Frame: Click on the "Past" button to gain insights into past events, experiences, or lessons that have influenced your journey. Click on the "Present" button to receive guidance and clarity on your current situation or challenges. Click on the "Future" button to explore potential outcomes, opportunities, or future paths.

  3. Receiving a Tarot Card: After selecting a time frame, click on the corresponding button. The Mystic Oracle web app will generate a random tarot card associated with the chosen time frame. The card's image, name, and a brief description will be displayed on the screen.

  4. Card Interpretation: Take a moment to observe the imagery, symbols, and colors on the card. Each element holds significance and contributes to the card's overall meaning. Read the provided description and interpretation of the card's symbolism, representing the energy and lessons related to your chosen time frame. Reflect on how the card's message may relate to your current circumstances or the question you seek answers to.

  5. Exploring Further: If you wish to explore additional cards or receive more readings, simply click on another time frame button (Past, Present, or Future). Each time you click a button, the Mystic Oracle web app will generate a new random card related to the selected time frame.

  6. Sharing and Saving: If you would like to share your card reading or save it for future reference, utilize the sharing options provided by your web browser. Use the app's screenshot functionality or copy the card's image and description to store or share it through your preferred platforms.
